Incident Report
This Incident Investigation Form should be used to record all incidents, including near misses, and may also be used to report hazards in the workplace.

The form should be completed as soon as possible following the incident. If the person involved cannot complete the form it may be completed on their behalf. Once the form has been completed it must be submitted to the Human Resources (HR) Manager and to the manager/Supervisor of the injured (or near injured) person.
